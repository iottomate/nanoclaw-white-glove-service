## Security-First by Design

Your bot never sees raw credentials. We use secure OAuth middleware ‚Äî all tokens are managed separately with a full audit trail and instant revoke capability. **Container sandboxing, firewall hardening, exec allowlists, and strict permission boundaries** keep your data safe.

**Privacy-first architecture:**
- Data stays on YOUR hardware, never cloud
- Container isolation for each WhatsApp group
- Read-only access between contexts
- Zero knowledge of your credentials
- Full audit trail of all actions

Unlike cloud AI services, your conversations and data never leave your infrastructure.
